# Workflow: SynthesisDigest

## Objective

Read across existing notes and produce a compact digest of patterns,
contradictions, knowledge gaps, and next actions.

## Difference From CompileKnowledge

- `CompileKnowledge` creates a reusable artifact for a topic or agent.
- `SynthesisDigest` creates a periodic or scoped digest of what the vault says.

## Inputs

- time range, such as `7d` or `30d`
- area, such as `resources`, `projects`, or `all`
- optional mode, such as `devil` for adversarial review

## Procedure

1. Select notes by scope.
2. Extract repeated claims and patterns.
3. Identify contradictions and stale assumptions.
4. Identify knowledge gaps.
5. Recommend next actions.
6. Save digest only after confirmation.

## Output Format

```md
# Synthesis Digest — YYYY-MM-DD

## Summary

## Patterns

## Contradictions

## Knowledge Gaps

## Next Actions

## Sources
```
