# Research Pack

Adds source-backed research workflows to Core.

Copy this pack into the vault root after installing Core.

## Includes

- `StandardResearch.md`
- agent role cards
- source-backed note template

## Trigger Guidance

- Claude / Gemini: broad research instructions may trigger the workflow.
- Codex: use explicit keywords such as `StandardResearch`, `research workflow`, or
  `investigation workflow` before dispatching subagents.
